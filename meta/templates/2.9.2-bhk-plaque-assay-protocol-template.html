D2: infecting cells (with concentrated virus)

Materials

- Reagents:

  • BHK media
  • 2% LMP agarose
  • 2X MEM

- Equipment:

  • water bath (56¬∞C)
  • 96-well plate
  • (optional) multichannel pipette
  • 6- or 12-well plates (# determined by cell count-based calc.)

- Species/Strains:

  • plated BHK cultures
  • designated concentrated viral titer

Procedure:

  1. Thaw concentrated viral titer(s)
    • disregard any excess virus; NO REFREEZE
  2. Label BHK plates with desired dilution series
    • e.g. (for 6-well plate) 0 ‚Üí -5, -1 ‚Üí -6, -2 ‚Üí -7
  3. Melt 2% LMP ‚Üí leave in 56¬∞C water bath
    • Bring 2X MEM to room temp
  4. Prepare 6 serial dilutions (per plate)
    • pipette 180 ¬µL BHK media (x6/plate)
    • add 20 ¬µL concentrated virus to 180 ¬µL media
    • mix with pipette aspiration & repeat for remaining series, sequentially transferring 20 ¬µL to designated wells
  5. Add 150 ¬µL of each dilution to appropriate well of 6-well plate
    • 100 ¬µL for 12-well plate
    • Rotate plate to evenly cover monolayer
    • Incubate @ 37¬∞C for 1hr (set timer!)
  6. After incubation, prepare 1:1 mix of 2X MEM and 2% LMP agarose
    • 3 mL/well (+ 2 mL for error)

    • (optional) add ~extra LMP to correct for viscosity
