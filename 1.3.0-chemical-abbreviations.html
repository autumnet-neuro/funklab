PBS ≡ phosphate buffered saline

non-toxic isotonic buffer solution

effectively remove unbound substances & reagents, while maintaining physiological conditions

used in:

... cell culture

  • as an isotonic solution to prevent rupturing or shriveling
  • as a nontoxic rinse to remove unwanted substances (e.g. media components, serum proteins)

... biochemical & cellular assays (e.g. Western blotting, ELISA)

  • as a diluent & washing buffer to maintain a stable physiological environment
  • with added Tween 20, as a blocking agent to prevent non-specific antibody binding
    • blocking: protein binds to all parts of membrane where target protein didn't before antibody is added to prevent non-specific binding

can be stored @ RT or refrigerator
