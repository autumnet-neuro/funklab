2.3.2 SYBR qPCR protocol

objective: quantify cDNA levels with SYBR green qPCR to monitor sequence amplification

Timeline: 3-4hrs

Prep:

  • Prepare cDNA stocks acc. RNA extraction + cDNA conversion protocols

  • Calculate # of samples to run (in triplicates) and how many target genes to analyze → determine amt of DNA and primers needed for completion

  • (# of primers) x (3 technical replicates/sample) + 2 for error = total# of reactions per sample

    • a no template control for each gene/tissue?

  • Ensure enough to run 10ng total DNA per reaction mixture

  • Note: mastermix + DNA can be at room temp. (ice not needed)

  • polymerase is a hot start

Materials:

Reagents:

  • SYBR 2x Supermix
  • Primers
  • Molecular grade H2O

Equipment:

  • Vortex mixer
  • Centrifuge
  • qPCR machine
  • multichannel pipette

Disposables:

  • RNAse free pipette tips
  • RNAse free 1.7mL (Eppendorf) microcentrifuge tubes
  • 384 well plate

Procedure:

  1. Thaw SYBR 2x Supermix and primers on the bench at room temperature.

  2. Dilute primers in molecular grade H2O to make 10uM stocks

a. Vortex mixture and then pulse spin in the centrifuge prior to use.

  1. Dilute cDNA in molecular grade H2O from 50ng/ul to 5ng/ul (1:10 dilution) to

have an accurate amount to pipette.

a. Once prepared, vortex to mix and then pulse spin in the centrifuge prior

to use

  1. Make SYBR Supermix in 1.7mL Eppendorf tubes for each of your gene targets (excl. cDNA)

instructions/layout.
